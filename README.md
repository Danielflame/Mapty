<h3><b>Project Name: Mapty</b></h3>

<b style="font-weight:bold;">How to Use</b>
1)	Click on your current location
2)	A form apperas
3)	Select either Runnin or Cycling
4)	Input the corresponding details
5)	Press Enter
6)	Your workout has been saved
